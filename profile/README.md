# Meralus Systems Group (MSG)
**Engineering Secure, Scalable, and Production-Ready Infrastructure.**

I maintain Meralus Systems Group (MSG) as a dedicated R&D lab to architect and stress-test enterprise automation frameworks. By simulating complex production constraints, MSG serves as a rigorous testing ground for Enterprise Hardening (CIS/STIG) and Ansible-driven infrastructure, ensuring all workflows are battle-hardened and validated before deployment.
